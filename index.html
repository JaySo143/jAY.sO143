The Super Mario Bros. Movie

The Super Mario Bros. Movie was released in theaters in April 7th, 2023. Directed by Aaron Horvath and Michael Jelenic, and created by Illumination and Nintendo.

The movie revolves with the well known plumber we all know and love, Mario, who embarks on an adventure with Princess Peach after his brother has been captured by Bowser and his minions.
